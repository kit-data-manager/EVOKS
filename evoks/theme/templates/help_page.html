Q&A

Create Vocabulary

You can find the "Create Vocabulary" button in the left sidebar. You can either import a vocabulary by uploading an RDF/Turtle file or create a new vocabulary by entering a vocabulary name. You can then further edit the vocabulary fields on the vocabulary details page. You can get there by clicking the newly created vocabulary on the Dashboard page.

Modify Vocabulary and Terms

Terms and vocabularies can be changed on their detail pages. Either change the text in the input field and click "Update", or create a new field and enter it.

Create new prefix

To create a new prefix for a vocabulary, go to the Prefix page. You can find this page by clicking on the vocabulary in the Dashboard. You then get to the vocabularies Detail page. Under the search bar there are five tabs, one of them is called "Prefix". When you click on it, you can add prefixes in the large text box. The changes will not be applied until you click "Save".

Create Team

You can create a new team by clicking the "Create Team" button in the left sidebar. Then a pop-up window will open where you enter a team name and click "Create". Clicking "Teams" in the sidebar will take you to a page listing all the teams you belong to. Find the newly created team and click on it. You will find a listing of all members and an input field to add members by their mail address. You can kick members by clicking on „kick“ in the listing.
